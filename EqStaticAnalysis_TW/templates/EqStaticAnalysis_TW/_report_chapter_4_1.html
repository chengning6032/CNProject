A. 計算原始比值:$\displaystyle{ \dfrac{S_{aD,X}}{F_{uD,X}} = \dfrac{ {{ results.SaDX|floatformat:4 }} }{ {{ results.FuDX|floatformat:4 }} } = {{ results.ratio_SaDX_FuDX|floatformat:4 }} }$
B. 依式(2-2)修正:
$$ \left( \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} \right)_m = \begin{cases} \displaystyle \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} & ; \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} \le 0.3 \\ \displaystyle 0.52 \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} + 0.144 & ; 0.3 < \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} < 0.8 \\ \displaystyle 0.70 \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} & ; \dfrac{S_{aD}}{F_u} \ge 0.8 \end{cases} $$
